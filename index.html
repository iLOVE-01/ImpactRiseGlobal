Welcome

Welcome to Impact Rise Global (IRG) official website, where we educate, lead, innovate, and accelerate the impact of the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) in Africa and globally.

About Us

Name: The Impact Rise Global (IRG)

Tagline: Empowering Global Changemakers locally.

Founded: December 2023

Founder & CEO: Kehinde John Okunade

Headquarters / Base: Ibadan, Nigeria (but with global reach ambitions)

Nature: Youth-led, non-profit, social impact organization

Core Focus Areas: Education, leadership, innovation, and accelerating the impact of the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) in Africa and globally

Our vision and mission

Vision: A world where young people are informed, inspired, and empowered to lead impactful change across education, innovation, sustainability, social development and SDG goals locally, regionally, and globally.

Mission:

  • Equip youth with leadership, problem-solving, and civic engagement skills.
  • Implement innovative projects that address SDG goals in education, climate action, and social equality.
  • Create opportunities for cross-border youth collaboration, learning, and mentorship.
  • Inspire a generation of changemakers capable of scaling impact beyond their immediate communities.
  • Accelerate the development of SDG goals glocally.
