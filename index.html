Welcome

Welcome to Impact Rise Global (IRG) official website, where we educate, lead, innovate, and accelerate the impact of the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) in Africa and globally.

About Us

Name: The Impact Rise Global (IRG)

Tagline: Empowering Global Changemakers locally.

Founded: December 2023

Founder & CEO: Kehinde John Okunade

Headquarters / Base: Ibadan, Nigeria (but with global reach ambitions)

Nature: Youth-led, non-profit, social impact organization

Core Focus Areas: Education, leadership, innovation, and accelerating the impact of the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) in Africa and globally

Our vision and mission

Vision: A world where young people are informed, inspired, and empowered to lead impactful change across education, innovation, sustainability, social development and SDG goals locally, regionally, and globally.

Mission:

  • Equip youth with leadership, problem-solving, and civic engagement skills.
  • Implement innovative projects that address SDG goals in education, climate action, and social equality.
  • Create opportunities for cross-border youth collaboration, learning, and mentorship.
  • Inspire a generation of change-makers capable of scaling impact beyond their immediate communities.
  • Accelerate the development of SDG goals globally.

Values & Principles

  • Youth empowerment
  • Education for all
  • Leadership development
  • Innovation and creative problem-solving
  • Collaboration and global perspective
  • Integrity, inclusiveness, and ethical action

Target Audience & Beneficiaries


Primary Beneficiaries:

  • Students in secondary and tertiary institutions in Nigeria (world-wide focused school tours)
  • Young people globally via digital platforms, workshops, and international collaborations

Estimated Reach (so far):
  • Direct beneficiaries: 300-500 students from multiple schools in Ibadan during initial school tours
  • Indirect beneficiaries: potentially 1,000+ via social media campaigns, collaborations, and media exposure

Inclusiveness:
  • Youth aged 16-35
  • Global recruitment for advisory boards and collaborations (at least one representative from each continent)

Our programs & Initiatives


Impact Rise School Tours
  • Visits to secondary schools across Ibadan (and later across Nigeria).
  • Workshops on leadership, SDGs, innovation, and civic engagement.
  • Hands-on activities, mentorship, and mini-project challenges.
  • Goal: Inspire students to take tangible action in their communities.
